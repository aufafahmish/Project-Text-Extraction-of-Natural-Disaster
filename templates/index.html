From News to Action

We extract and analyze natural disaster news articles in Indonesia to provide accurate and timely information. With the right data, we can enhance awareness, preparedness, and disaster response.

What Are Natural Disasters?

Natural disasters are extreme and sudden events caused by environmental factors that result in significant damage to life, property, and the ecosystem.

Types of Natural Disaster:

  • Flood (Banjir)
  • Landslide (Tanah Longsor)
  • Earthquake (Gempa Bumi)
  • Eruption (Gunung Meletus)
  • Hurricane (Puting Beliung)
  • Extreme Weather (Cuaca Ekstrem)
  • Tsunami

Disaster Reports in Real-Time

How does this work?

Disaster news is scraped in real-time, and its content is then extracted using classification techniques to identify the disaster name and impact, while the disaster category, location, date, and time are extracted using Named Entity Recognition (NER) techniques.
By extracting disaster news, we rapidly convert raw information into structured data, enabling quick response in assessing disaster impacts
